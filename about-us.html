Our Story

About ProDuplicator

Engineering the standard for professional physical media duplication, high-capacity testing parameters, and certified forensic data sanitization frameworks since 1997.

Nearly Three Decades of Data Fidelity

Founded during the early evolution of storage formats, ProDuplicator set out to fill a critical technological gap: creating reliable hardware configurations that copy data without requiring an un-secure host computer environment.

Today, we design standalone replication equipment used across corporate IT infrastructures, defense contractors, production houses, and medical fields. From classic optical discs to high-velocity M.2 NVMe SSD arrays, our core mission remains unchanged—delivering exact bit-for-bit hardware duplication arrays that operate flawlessly under continuous industrial schedules.

Our Core Pillars

The engineering principles that guide every configuration we manufacture and support.

Bit-Level Accuracy

Data corruption is non-negotiable. Our equipment processes raw bit-by-bit cloning matrix structures, verified through automated cyclic redundancy checks (CRC) to guarantee perfect mirror integrity every run.

Standalone Isolation

By deploying entirely closed-system architectures, our duplicators remain natively immune to malware injections, operating system driver conflicts, or internal network vulnerabilities.

Direct Engineering Support

We back every system setup with real, domestic technical assistance specialists. You interface directly with hardware specialists who understand complex deployment parameters.
